Why have a website?

Having a website is important, even if you don't provide any service online. A website is fetched by search engines and can increase the reach among the audience for your subject.

Why focus on web design?

A website can often be the first impression of your subject to your audience. A bland website can often come looking rather unprofessional as opposed to a well designed one.
